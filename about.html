About Me

¡Hola y bienvenidos! I am Elena, a 19-year-old ambitious woman who, with a suitcase full of passion and a camera in hand, crossed oceans to pursue my dreams. In the heart of Chicago, I am carving my path as a model. The world of fashion has become my playground, providing a canvas to express myself through the art of posing and styling. From the beautiful streets of Spain to the cosmopolitan cityscapes of the U.S., each step on the runway tells a story of cultural fusion and individuality. I navigated the intricate world of fashion collaborating with clothing brands and participating in fashion shows and runways allowed me to refine my craft, cultivating a signature blend of elegance and streetwear, absorbing the rich influences that shaped my unique style.
